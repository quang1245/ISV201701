Activities

Explore Cat Ba

Cat Ba is an island located in Lan Ha Bay, recognized by UNESCO as a World Natural Heritage site. You can engage in activities such as visiting Lan Ha Bay, exploring Sung Sot Cave, visiting Cat Ba National Park, swimming at Cat Co Beach, or joining a cruise tour on the bay.

Visit the Cao Son Historical and Cultural Complex

Cao Son is a historical and cultural site located in Tien Lang District, Hai Phong. It preserves the cultural and architectural heritage of ancient Vietnamese people, including temples, city gates, village gates, wooden bridges, and caves.
